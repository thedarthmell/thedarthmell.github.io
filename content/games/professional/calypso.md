**Calypso: Brethren of the Coast** is a cooperative third-person shooter (TPS) developed by **Madbyte Games** in Unreal Engine 5. Set in a post-apocalyptic world completely dominated by open seas, players team up to raid massive ships and navigate the complex relationships between surviving factions.

### My Role: Lead Game Designer

I established the foundational design of *Calypso* from the ground up during its core development phase... Working in Unreal Engine 5 and directing a team consisting of a junior and a beginner game designer, I built the systemic architecture that the game's entire structure would ultimately be constructed upon.

**Key Responsibilities & Contributions:**
* **Foundational System Design:** Architected the core gameplay loops and general systems, establishing a robust design foundation from scratch.
* **Faction & Quest Architecture:** Designed the dynamic faction and community systems. Created the overarching quest structure where players receive targeted heist contracts from specific factions (e.g., raiding a rival faction's ship to retrieve high-value assets).
* **Character Development:** Conceptualized the core characters, detailing their backgrounds, specific traits, and unique features to seamlessly integrate them into the lore of a flooded world.
* **Design Leadership:** Mentored and managed two other designers, guiding their workflows and ensuring the foundational design documentation was scalable for the rest of the production pipeline.
